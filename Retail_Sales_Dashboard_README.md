# 📊 Retail Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
The Retail Sales Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI. It provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales performance by combining executive KPIs, geographical insights, customer behavior, and product performance into an easy-to-use dashboard.

The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, identify sales trends, analyze customer demographics, and evaluate product performance using interactive filters and navigation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall retail sales performance.
- Monitor important business KPIs.
- Identify high-performing states and sales channels.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate product performance.
- Enable interactive analysis through slicers and page navigation.

---

### 3️⃣ Customer & Product Insights
